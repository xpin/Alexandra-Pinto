Research Interests

My main research interests are in the fields of Autonomic and Power-Aware runtime solutions in Parallel Computing and in High-Level Parallel Programming abstractions.
For my Ph.D. research, I considered scenarios where the application user wants to express specific Quality of Service (QoS) requirements on his application, in terms of performance and/or power consumption. The main contribution of my work have been the design of different online algorithms that, without any previous knowledge about the application, are able to dynamically and automatically change the number of resources allocated to the application (i.e. its configuration) to enforce such requirements. This can imply changing the number of cores allocated to the application, scaling their frequency or modifying the allocation of threads over physical resources. I also implemented such concepts by providing a full software stack, available as open source, which can be used to enforce explicit performance and power consumption requirements on parallel applications.
